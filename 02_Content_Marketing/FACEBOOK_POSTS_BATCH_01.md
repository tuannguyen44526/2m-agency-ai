### 🇺🇸 TIẾNG ANH

**Thinking about adding a deck this summer? Here's what most homeowners don't ask — but should. 👇**

As a general contractor who builds decks regularly in North Alabama, we see the same questions come up after the fact. Save yourself the headache:

**1. Ask about the permit.**  
In Huntsville and Madison County, most deck projects require a permit. A contractor who skips this is leaving you with a liability — not a backyard upgrade.

**2. Composite vs. pressure-treated wood — know the difference.**  
Composite costs more upfront but lasts longer and needs almost zero maintenance. Pressure-treated is more affordable but needs annual sealing in our Alabama heat. We'll walk you through the tradeoffs.

**3. Don't just price the deck — price the drainage.**  
Water management under and around your deck matters. Cut corners here and you'll have problems in 2-3 years.

At 2M Construction, we build decks the right way — and we'll answer every question you have before we start.
